A few things worth remembering

  • Books on the Hill is the job to go for first — walk in, don't apply online
  • Your bus stop is The Ancient Briton on Harpenden Road — six routes, right on the doorstep
  • Apply for your provisional driving licence early — it takes about a week to arrive by post
  • Sign up to mailing lists for the London institutions — events come round fast
  • Say yes to things, even when you're not sure. That's how you find your people
  • If you think you might be overtaxed, check your tax code — it happens a lot in first jobs
